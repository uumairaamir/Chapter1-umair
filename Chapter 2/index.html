Overview

"Suits" is a legal drama series that follows talented college drop-out Mike Ross, who lands a job as a law associate for Harvey Specter, despite never having attended law school. The series explores their partnership and the challenges they face in the legal world.

Key Information

Genre: Legal Drama
Created by: Aaron Korsh
First Released: June 23, 2011
Seasons: 9 (final season aired in 2019)
Website: Suits on USA Network

Cast

  • Gabriel Macht as Harvey Specter
  • Patrick J. Adams as Mike Ross
  • Meghan Markle as Rachel Zane
  • Rick Hoffman as Louis Litt
  • Gina Torres as Jessica Pearson

Characters

The series features a dynamic cast of characters, including:

  • Harvey Specter: A top corporate lawyer known for his confidence and charm.
  • Mike Ross: A brilliant but unlicensed lawyer with a photographic memory.
  • Rachel Zane: A paralegal and Mike's love interest.
  • Louis Litt: A senior partner with a complex personality.

Reviews

"Suits" has been praised for its sharp writing, engaging characters, and stylish presentation. It has garnered a loyal fan base and received several award nominations throughout its run.
